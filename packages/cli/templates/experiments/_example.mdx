# Example experiment

The unit of the loop is the **idea / prototype** — a hypothesis you validate with
real data. What change are you testing, who for (`icp`), on what surface
(`section`), and what metric tells you it worked? Write the rationale here.

`/init-experiment` creates one of these · `/write-variants` builds the prototype ·
`/measure` wires the PostHog funnel · `/close-experiment` records the result and
appends the learning to `experiments/LEARNINGS.md`.
