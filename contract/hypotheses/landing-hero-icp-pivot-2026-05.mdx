## Why This Hypothesis

The current hero leads with component drift — a design-engineer concern. D3 resolved (2026-05-07): the primary ICP is the pre-PMF founder vibe-coding with Claude Code or Cursor, running GTM experiments, using PostHog. Their core pain is not "my component is wrong in Figma" — it is "I made a product decision from memory and I have no record of what I tested or why."

The control framing positions Systemix as a design system correctness tool. Variant B positions it as a hypothesis validation loop — you shipped something, Hermes reads the signals, you close the loop. One click. Decision written back. The next experiment starts from known ground.

## Variants

**Control** (`control`): "Every component is a guess until production proves it."
Design-engineer framing. Emotion: correctness, trust, anti-drift.

**Variant B** (`variant_b`): "You shipped it. Did it work? Close the loop."
Founder framing. Emotion: action, signal, closure. The loop is the product.
