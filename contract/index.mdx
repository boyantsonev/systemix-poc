## Brief

This instance is Systemix running on its own contract. The team is one founder;
the primary ICP is the **pre-PMF founder** who ships daily with agents (decided
2026-05-07, superseding the earlier design-engineer framing). The engine's job:
pursue the goals below, ship variants, read the signals, synthesize evidence
with Hermes, route decisions through the queue, and write what it learns back
into this document.

The design system is not the product here — it is one of the goals. The loop is
the product: ship → signals → synthesis → decision → written back → the next
ship starts from evidence.

## Autonomy

One dial, three levels: **ghost** proposes everything and writes nothing alone;
**assisted** writes the low-risk changes and proposes the rest; **autonomous**
writes most things. This instance is at **ghost** — every write the engine wants
to make arrives as a proposal in the decision queue, and nothing lands without a
human approve.

The matrix below is what the code enforces, not a description of it — the write
sites consult the same policy, and the column highlighted is the current level.
The track record is the receipts: every decision a human has resolved, by outcome.

<AutonomyClause />

The covenant: **humans give goals.** At no level does the engine self-assign
one — goals and the brief are *propose* in every column. Raising the level is
itself a decision that lands in the queue.
