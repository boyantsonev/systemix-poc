## Brief

This instance is Systemix running on its own contract. The team is one founder;
the primary ICP is the **pre-PMF founder** who ships daily with agents (decided
2026-05-07, superseding the earlier design-engineer framing). The engine's job:
pursue the goals below, ship variants, read the signals, synthesize evidence
with Hermes, route decisions through the queue, and write what it learns back
into this document.

The design system is not the product here — it is one of the goals. The loop is
the product: ship → signals → synthesis → decision → written back → the next
ship starts from evidence.

## Autonomy

Current execution tier: **trust 0 — ghost**. Every write the engine wants to
make arrives as a proposal in the decision queue; nothing lands without a human
approve. Within that gate, `hermes.autonomy: balanced` (the strip above renders
the live values) governs how Hermes routes by confidence — high-confidence
synthesis is queued as a recommendation, low-confidence as a question.

| What the engine may write | ghost (today) | balanced | high |
|---|---|---|---|
| Evidence fields on hypotheses | propose | auto-write | auto-write |
| New hypothesis drafts | propose | draft + propose run | auto-create within budget |
| Memory entries | propose | propose | auto-append (provenance required) |
| The brief | propose | propose | propose |
| Goals | propose | propose | propose |

The covenant: **humans give goals.** At no tier does the engine self-assign
one. Raising the tier is itself a decision that lands in the queue.
