## Why this goal

The velocity-gap thesis: founders now ship 10× faster with agents, but learning
is still manual and a quarter behind, so speed doesn't compound. The landing
must prove this framing converts the people who feel that gap — pre-PMF
founders shipping daily — before it becomes the company's lead story.
