**Status: proposed.** This fifth goal type is pending confirmation by the
operator — it is parked and runs nothing until confirmed.

**Done means:** one audit cycle over the configured window produces at least
one improvement proposal — a skill tweak, a threshold change, a contract
amendment — accepted by a human through the queue.

**Kill if:** two audit cycles in a row produce nothing a human accepts. An
engine that audits itself into noise should stop auditing itself.

## Why this goal

The instance config already carries a `self_improvement` block (audit mode,
meta-contract path, audit window). This goal would activate it as a first-class
contract: the engine applying the same loop — evidence, synthesis, proposal,
human decision — to its own operation. The covenant holds here too: it proposes
amendments; humans approve them.
