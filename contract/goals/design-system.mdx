**Done means:** the records show zero drifted tokens and zero drifted
components, and the Figma Variables stay in sync with `globals.css` (a clean
bridge diff) after every sync run. This goal is never "finished" — it is held.

**Kill if:** drift recurs within a week of a sync, three sync cycles in a row.
That failure mode means the pipeline's design is wrong, not the tokens —
escalate an architecture review of the sync loop instead of re-running it.

## Why this goal

This is the engine's infrastructure duty — the original Systemix product,
reframed as one goal among five instead of the whole surface. The design system
is the means: every variant the other goals ship renders through these tokens
and components, so they have to be true. Drift here silently falsifies
everything built on top.
