**Done means:** five moderated walkthroughs of the founder-loop prototype in
the Atlas, with at least four participants narrating the loop — define → ship →
signals → decision — unprompted. Every comprehension failure gets filed as a
hypothesis under this goal, not noted and forgotten.

**Kill if:** after the fifth walkthrough, founders still cannot say why they
would come back tomorrow. That means the loop reads as a feature rather than a
product, and the fix is positioning, not prototype polish.

## Why this goal

This is the rung toward the long arc: an engine that creates and validates
agentic experiences through data, signals, and user research. The Atlas
prototypes are the research instrument — clickable workflows rendered in the
instance's own design system, cheap to revise, honest to test.

## State of play

The founder-loop and founder-init workflows render as click-through prototypes
in the Atlas today. The research wiring — sessions, notes, evidence written
back to this contract — is the new work this goal demands.
